About The Event

About The Event

Overview

The Ministry of Veterans of The Liberation Struggle Affairs (MoVLSA), Young Achievers Zimbabwe (YAZ), and the Zimbabwe Economic Development Conference (ZEDCON) are organizing a conference to officially open the Manhize Iron and Steel company in Midlands Province, Zimbabwe.

Guest of Honor

President E.D. Mnangagwa will be the guest of honor, and the President of the Holding Company for Manhize will also be in attendance.

About Manhize

Manhize, a recently established and substantial iron and steel company, has invested in cutting-edge facilities and technologies, and is committed to sustainable production and economic development. The company is expected to create thousands of jobs.

Conference Highlights

  • Showcase of the company's innovations
  • Engagement with stakeholders
  • Discussion of industry challenges and opportunities
  • Promotion of collaboration
